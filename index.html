MentorMatch uses AI to help you

Mission
Promote gender equality in tech through mentorship.
Vision
Build a global, inclusive, AI-driven mentorship community
Why Mentorship Matters
Mentorship is a powerful tool for personal and professional growth,
especially for people of marginalized genders. Mentors can help you
learn faster, build confidence, expand your network, and feel included.
